| Jon the journey man |

“Someone who really wants to get in a little over his head and make a dent in the universe.” - Steve Jobs

The quote from the late and greatly missed Steve Jobs has been something I have always related to and find myself fulfilling through all lifes endeavors.

My journey is unlike any other. Growing up with my grandmother I was a quiet kid who followed directions and never had trouble with school. In high school I had many interests and thought my life could go in many different directions; engineer, entrepreneur, architect, chef, and the list goes on. Oh boy, I right in the fact that my life would be unique.

I won a full-tuition scholarship to attend Scottsdale Culinary Institute in a state-wide competition in Arizona. I graduated with my culinary degree. I was a chef and pastry chef at 4 star restaurants. But I was not satisfied and knew I was meant to do more.

I went back to school for business. I attended nationally top ranked Entrepreneurship and Business Management programs at the University of Arizona. I got my degree and double majored. I was a General Manager and things were good, but too easy. Again, I was not satisfied and knew I was meant to do more.

I was called into the military and enlisted in the U.S. Army as an Information Technology Specialist. I had always tinkered around with gadgets, like tube televisions, gameboys, pagers, POTS phones, and so on… I thought this was something that should be more than a hobby. I instantly excelled, I deployed, I was an leader of soldiers, and gained many technical certifications. I switched from a soldier to an officer. I was selected to Cyber, and learned even more. Then I became a developer and certified as a Cyberspace Capabilities Developer. That is where I finally felt I was doing what I was called to do all along.

Being a developer is a culmination of everything I enjoy doing and learning. I have found my passion on this earth and now am being called to transition out of the military and take my talents to the world and develop solutions to world problems.

I am poised to “make a dent in the universe”.

| Jon the family man |

My family is my heart and soul. I have a way out of my class wife whom we share four perfect children together. To round out our home we have two yorkies and a parrotlet. We are a big family that has lots of love, support, and talents to share with others. We are firm in our faith and wish nothing more than to make a positive impact on the world and those whom we are blessed to encounter on this journey.
